# Fingriffin Editorial Guidelines

## Audience

- Professionals
- Entrepreneurs
- Investors
- ESOP holders
- SME owners

## Style

- Analytical
- Story-driven
- Practical

## Avoid

- Clickbait
- Stock tips
- Market predictions
- Sensational headlines

## Preferred Structure

1. Observation
2. Personal Experience
3. Analysis
4. Framework
5. Actionable Takeaway

## Tone

- Honest
- Reflective
- Evidence-based
- Long-term oriented
